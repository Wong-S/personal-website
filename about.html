About Me

I'm a biologist who transitioned into Software Engineering.

My first exposure to programming was with R, where I became fascinated with its use to tackle complex problems. Since then, I have been motivated to learn other technological languages used to build meaningful products and services for people. I have seen the benefits of technology in the science industry, and want to be at the forefront of this technological innovation by building systems and features to help organizations reach their goals.

Currently, I'm looking for a junior developer role (open to all roles) where I can contribute, collaborate and take on leadership responsibilities. While my primary experience is with a full-stack technology, I am driven to continue my developer growth by gaining more experience using different frameworks and designs in the front-end and back-end. Additionally, I am very curious and eager to learn more about the infrastructure and architectural design to maintain sites at a large scale.

Please feel free to connect with me on social media or contact me for more information.
